### Reynolds
$$ \text{Re} = \frac{\nu D \rho}{\mu} $$

#### Viskosity
$$ \nu = 4 \cdot 10^{-3} \text{Pa}$$
#### Density
$$ \rho = 1 \frac{g}{cm^3} $$
#### Diameter
$$ d = 3 \cdot 10^{-6} - 3 \cdot 10^{-2}$$
#### Velocity
$$ v = 0.25 - 1 \frac{m}{s}$$
