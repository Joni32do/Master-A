- $q = q_w + q_x i + q_y j + q_z k$

- $$ij = -ji = k, jk = -kj = i, ki = ik = j$$
- $$ijk = -1$$
- Inverse is just negation $q^{-1} = q_w - q_x i - q_y j - q_z k$

- Rotation of $v_q$ by $q$ is
- $$ v'+q = q v_q q^{-1}$$
